Matt's Favorite Restaurants in Charlotte, NC

  2. Billy Jack's Shack
  3. North Italia

About the Restuarants:

  • 1. Saigon Palace

    A family owned restaurant in South Charlotte serving up Authentic Vietnamese Cuisine.

  • 2. Billy Jack's Shack

    Slinging Burgers, Bacon, Bird, and Beers in the heart of NoDa. Serving up a ten napkin burger and sticky nuggs or hangout on a weekend for one of the best brunch menus around.

  • 3. North Italia

    Traditional Italian cooking using the best ingredients to create and serve crave-worthy food. Serving their chef's daily choice pizza and freshly made pasta.

  • 4. Tacos El Nevado

    A small place where you can enjoy the authentic mexican flavor. A family that makes their food with authentic mexican flavor, all while serving up traditional dishes.

  • 5. Seoul Food Meat Company

    A rustic hangout for Korean-inspired BBQ eats paired with Asian craft beers & custom cocktails.
