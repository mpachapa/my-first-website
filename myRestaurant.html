  • 3. Original Pancake House

    Chain serving signature pancakes & other American breakfast classics all day in dinerlike digs.


  • 4. Inizio Pizza Napoletana

    Traditional Neapolitan-style pizzas headline the menu at this hip hangout with a patio.


  • 5. Mama Ricotta's

    Traditional Italian cooking & New Haven-style pizza in a warm, roomy setting with a covered patio.
